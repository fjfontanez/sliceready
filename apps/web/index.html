SliceReady

Fix broken STL and 3MF meshes so they slice and print. Built for the models AI generators hand you half-finished.

[✓] Everything runs in your browser. Your model never leaves your computer — no upload, no account, no server.

Drop in an STL or 3MF. SliceReady closes open edges, corrects flipped normals and removes degenerate triangles, then tells you exactly what it changed — and what it could not fix. Take the repaired STL straight to Cura, OrcaSlicer, Bambu Studio or PrusaSlicer.

Built by the team behind SliceMargin. Guides — why your model won’t print NO UPLOAD · NO ACCOUNT · NO TRACKING
